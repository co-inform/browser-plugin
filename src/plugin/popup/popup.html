About the Co-Inform Browser Plugin

Misinformation online generates misperceptions. By bringing together a multidisciplinary team of researchers and experts in computer science, behavioural science, and social science, Co-Inform aims at engaging all stakeholders in fighting misinformation by providing them with the tools to identify ‘fake news’ online, understand how they spread, and provide them with verified information.

To this end, Co-Inform has developed the Browser Plugin, a tool to raise citizens’ awareness of misinforming content, of related fact-checking articles and corrective information. As a proof of concept, the Co-Inform plugin has been developed as a browser extension focused on Twitter content.

More information about the project, the team, and the tool can be found on the Co-Inform web site.
